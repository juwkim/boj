<p>2022 is a year of the Winter Olympics! Curling has been one of the most popular winter sports as it requires skill, strategy, and sometimes a bit of luck.</p>

<p>In a curling game, two teams compete by sliding heavy granite stones on a long ice sheet. We call the teams the <i>red</i> team and the <i>yellow</i> team, as their stones are usually distinguished by the red and the yellow handle color.
